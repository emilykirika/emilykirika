about me

Do you have self-managed NDIS funding for speech therapy for your little one? Get in touch using the form below, and I would love to work with you! I have a NDIS worker screening check and have worked in both not-for-profit and private sector speech therapy roles before.

I am an independent speech pathologist based in South-East Melbourne (Dandenong) who specialises in working with children.

I am an experienced Speech Pathologist, originally from the UK, and have worked widely in the healthcare sector (both professionally and through my own lived experience). I have a deep understanding of the challenges that families face. I have a Masters (MSc) in Language Pathology from Newcastle University in the UK (a highly competitive placement-based degree program) and a Bachelors in Psychology (Kingston University, London, UK).

Key skills:
1) Interpersonal communication: working closely as part of multi-disciplinary teams (a team of many healthcare professionals), liaising with teaching staff, parents and other health professionals.
2) Compassionate: taking time to listen to family members who are distressed or concerned about their child’s progression.
3) Adaptable: adjusting sessions at short notice to get the most out of the child.
